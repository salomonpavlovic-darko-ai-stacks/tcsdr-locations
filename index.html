Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to repair a sliding glass door?

Roller replacement typically runs $185-395 per door in Florida, track repair/replacement $225-550, glass replacement $350-1,200+ depending on impact rating, and full hurricane-impact door replacement $1,800-4,500+. Most repairs include a free on-site estimate. Same-day service available across the Treasure Coast.

How long does a sliding door roller replacement take?

Typical tandem roller replacement takes 45-90 minutes per door. We bring replacement rollers for all major brands (PGT, CGI, Eagle, Andersen, Milgard, Jeld-Wen) and do the full job on-site in one visit, including door lift-out, new roller install, track cleaning, and alignment calibration.

Why is my sliding door so hard to open?

The most common cause is worn rollers - after 5-10 years of salt-air exposure, stock rollers seize, pit, or crack. Other causes: bent track, debris in the track channel, door out of plumb (foundation settling), broken handle mechanism, or failed bottom guide. All are repairable without replacing the full door.

Can you repair a sliding door track without replacing the door?

Yes. For minor damage, we use track cap overlays (stainless steel) or section replacements. For moderate damage, we replace the full bottom track while keeping your existing door, glass, and jambs. Full track replacement typically runs $225-550 and takes 2-3 hours.

Do you service hurricane impact sliding doors in Florida?

Yes. We repair and install impact-rated sliding doors meeting FL HVHZ code and Miami-Dade NOA, including PGT WinGuard, CGI Targa, Eagle E-Class, Andersen A-Series coastal, and Vetter hurricane-rated doors. Full product approval documentation provided for permit applications and insurance discounts.

Do you do commercial storefront door repair?

Yes. Commercial services include aluminum storefront door repair, automatic sliding door service (Stanley, Horton, Dorma, Nabco), panic hardware (Von Duprin, Detex, Adams Rite), closers (LCN, Norton), pivots, and bottom rails. Emergency commercial response available for break-ins and storm damage.

What sliding door brands do you service?

All major FL brands: PGT, CGI, Eagle, Andersen, Milgard, Jeld-Wen, Custom Windows, Vetter, Weather Shield, Pella, Therma-Tru, Marvin, Fleetwood, Western Window Systems, Kolbe, LaCantina, Euro-Wall, NanaWall, Quantum, Lawson, ES Windows, and Euroview. Non-standard brands accommodated with custom roller kits.

How do I know if my sliding door rollers are bad?

Signs of failing rollers: door is hard to push, scrapes when sliding, makes grinding/clicking noise, rattles in the track, rides low/scrapes threshold, leaves scratch marks on the track, or jumps the track. Rollers typically last 5-10 years in Florida salt air. Free inspection and diagnosis included with any service call.
